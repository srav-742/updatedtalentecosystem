# User Guide: How to use TalentEcoSystem

This guide walks you through the step-by-step process for both Job Seekers and Recruiters on the platform.

---

## üåü For Job Seekers

### Step 1: Sign Up & Profile Setup
- Navigate to the **Signup** page.
- Choose **Job Seeker** as your role.
- Complete your profile with your skills, experience, and education.

### Step 2: Finding a Job
- Go to the **Browse Jobs** section.
- Use filters to find roles that match your expertise.
- Click on **View Details** to read the full requirement.

### Step 3: The Application Process
Once you click **Apply**, you will enter the AI Pipeline:
1.  **Resume Upload**: Upload your latest resume (PDF). Wait for the AI to analyze your match score.
2.  **Assessment**: Complete the dynamic MCQs and Coding challenges. Ensure your webcam is on for proctoring.
3.  **AI Interview**: Click "Start Interview." Speak clearly into your microphone when prompted. The AI will ask you questions based on your background and the job.

### Step 4: Tracking Your Progress
- Visit **My Applications** to see your scores.
- Check the status (Applied, Under Review, Shortlisted, or Rejected).

---

## üíº For Recruiters

### Step 1: Setting up your Workspace
- Sign up as a **Recruiter**.
- Enter your company details in the **Profile** section.

### Step 2: Posting a Job
- Go to **Post a Job**.
- Enter the title, description, and *most importantly*, the skills required. The AI uses these skills to generate the candidate's assessment.

### Step 3: Managing Candidates
- Go to **Dashboard** or **Applicants**.
- You will see a list of all candidates who applied.
- **Review Scores**: Look at the "Final Score" (Average of Resume + Assessment + Interview).
- **Deep Dive**: Click on a candidate to see their interview transcript and specific skill gaps identified by AI.

### Step 4: Shortlisting
- Move candidates to "Shortlisted" to notify them of the next steps in your internal process.
